About Me

Do you need to write a text with a specific word or character limit? Do you want to change the case of your text easily? If yes, then you need this app! This app is a simple and powerful tool that can help you with your writing tasks. You can use it to:


  • Count the number of characters and words in your text. You can also see the average word length and sentence length.

  • Convert your text into upper case, lower case, title case, or sentence case. You can also toggle between these options with a single tap.

  • Copy, paste, share, or save your text with ease. You can also export your text as a PDF or a TXT file.

This app is fast, accurate, and easy to use. It works offline and does not require any permissions or subscriptions. It is compatible with all devices and platforms. Whether you are writing an essay, a tweet, a blog post, or a caption, this app can help you save time and improve your writing. Download it now and try it for yourself!
